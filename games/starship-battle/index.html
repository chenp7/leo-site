Starship Battle

⛶ Open Full Screen ← Back to Games

About

A fast-paced 2D space shooter built with HTML5 Canvas. Pilot your starship, dodge enemy fire, and survive 10 waves of alien attackers. Spend score on upgrades and allies between waves. How long can you last?

Controls

  • WASD Move relative to cursor
  • Space Boost
  • Mouse Aim & Shoot (left click)
  • 1 EMP Blast
  • 2 Time Slow
  • 3 Orbital Strike
  • Mobile: Single left stick controls direction + speed, with on-screen FIRE/BOOST/ability buttons

Details

  • Engine: HTML5 Canvas
  • Waves: 10
  • Enemy types: 7
  • Ally types: 4
  • Weapons: 5
