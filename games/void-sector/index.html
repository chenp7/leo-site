Void Sector

⛶ Open Full Screen ← Back to Games

About

A top-down space combat RPG. Capture enemy bases, build your fleet, find weapons and upgrades scattered across the sector, and defeat the Void Leviathan boss to win. Choose from 5 ships and 4 starting ally types.

Controls

  • WASD Fly
  • Space / Click Fire laser
  • Q Nova blast
  • F Interact with base
  • B Open shop
  • 1–5 Use item

Details

  • Engine: HTML5 Canvas
  • Ships: 5
  • Ally types: 4
  • Boss: Void Leviathan
  • Field items: 18+
